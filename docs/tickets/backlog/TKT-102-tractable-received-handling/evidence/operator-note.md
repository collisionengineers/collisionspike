`tractableexamples/` folder contains emails and pdf examples.

Tractable is an app used by Collision Engineers to obtain vehicle images.

The client takes photographs of the vehicle, which is then uploaded to a portal and e-mailed to Collision
Engineers.

The PDF contains:

Under "Vehicle Information":
- Vehicle Make
- Vehicle Model
- Year
- VIN
- Reg
- Mileage

Under "Submitted Vehicle Images":
- A variety of vehicle pictures that should include all requirements.
