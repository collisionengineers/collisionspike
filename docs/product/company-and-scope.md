# Company and scope

Collision Engineers receives vehicle-assessment work from insurers, solicitors, accident-management
companies, and trade sources. Instructions, documents, and images often arrive separately and must be
assembled without losing their source identity.

The product owns the path from intake through case assembly, review, readiness, EVA handoff, and Archive
filing. It also supports missing-information chasers, duplicate handling, provider-specific rules,
vehicle facts, and a complete audit trail.

The product boundary ends at the EVA handoff. Engineer inspection, report authoring, and delivery of the
finished engineering report remain outside this system.

The product is staff-only. It must show real case data, use handler language, keep uncertain matches for
human review, and fail conservatively rather than silently joining or discarding work.
