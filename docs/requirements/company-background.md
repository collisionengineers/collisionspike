## What they do

Collision Engineers is an **independent automotive engineering firm** specialising in vehicle
damage assessment, forensic analysis, and expert (court-compliant) reporting across the UK and
Europe. They serve legal professionals, insurers, accident-management companies, government
bodies, repairers/garages, and private clients — emphasising **independence** ("no commercial
bias") and **CPR-compliant** expert-witness work.

## Key services

- **Vehicle Damage Assessment** — accident damage reports (physical or desktop inspection),
  total-loss determination, pre-repair authorisation.
- **Forensic Engineering** — damage consistency, low-velocity-impact (LVI) causation,
  counter-fraud, scene reconstruction.
- **Vehicle Valuation Report** — market valuations (cars, motorcycles, commercial, bicycles).
- **Diminution in Value Report** — post-repair loss of value from real market data.
- **Roadworthy / Unroadworthy Report** — safety-compliance assessments.
- **Criminal Investigation Reports** — bespoke expert reports.
- **Bodyshop Estimates** — Audatex repair estimates via the **Repairer Portal** (£100 + VAT).

## Clients & intake channels

- Clients: solicitors / legal, insurers & accident-management companies, repairers/garages
  (via the Repairer Portal), government bodies, private clients.
- Channels: **New-business email** (newbusiness@collisionengineers.co.uk), **telephone**
  (0151 — Liverpool/North-West), **WhatsApp**, the **Repairer Portal**, and an **automated API**
  (Audatex) for established customers.

## Terminology used across the system

- **Provider** — a client/source that sends cases (insurer, solicitor, accident-management co.).
- **Principal** — Collision Engineers' internal 4-character code for each provider.
- **Case/PO** — `Principal + 2-digit year + 3-digit provider case number` (e.g. `CCPY26050`).
- Report types: engineer/assessment report, valuation, diminution, roadworthy, forensic.
